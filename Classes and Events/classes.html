Classes and Events

Explore a world of creativity at our maker space, where innovation knows no bounds! With our diverse range of classes including 3D printing, metal sculpting, and arts and crafts, there's something for every maker. We also have upcoming classes in wood sculpting, laser cutting, and web design! Enhance learning opportunities with our variety of packages and events. Join us on a journey of discovery and hands-on learning, and unleash your potential today!

3D PRINTING

Explore the world of 3D printing in our class designed for ages 10 and up! Join us for an exciting journey. Learn the basics of 3D modeling, discover how to operate 3D printers, and unleash your creativity as you bring your designs to life. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced maker, this class offers hands-on experience to help you master the art of 3D printing. Join today!

Age Requirements: 10+

When: Tuesdays and Thursdays

Time: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Where: Rm. 5

METAL WELDING

Welcome to our metal welding class! Join us every week for an immersive exploration of metalworks. Learn fundamental techniques such as welding, forging, and metal casting. Our expert instructors will provide hands-on guidance, ensuring a safe and rewarding experience for all skill levels. Don't miss this chance to ignite your passion! Join today!

Age Requirements: 16+

When: Mondays and Thursdays

Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Where: Rm. 4

ARTS AND CRAFTS

Join us for an exciting Arts and Crafts class suitable for all ages! Whether you're a child, teen, adult, or senior, you're welcome to unleash your creativity in this inclusive environment. Our classes are held every Saturday, offering a variety of hands-on projects and activities to explore different mediums such as painting, drawing, collage, and more. Come with us and let your imagination soar! Join today!

Age Requirements: All Ages

When: Saturdays

Time: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Where: Rm. 2

WOOD SCULPTING

Get inspired by our wood sculpting class suitable for ages 12 and above! Immerse yourself in the world of woodworking as you learn essential carving techniques and unleash your creativity. Whether you're a beginner or enthusiast, our experienced instructors will guide you through the process, from selecting the perfect wood to refining your carving skills.

Age Requirements: 12+

When: Wednesdays and Fridays

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Where: Rm. 3

CNC Machines

Calling all CNC fans. Anyone 16+ can join our CNC machines class. Absorb yourself in the programming side and program the cuts you want the machine to make! Get ready to use a range of machines from grinders and lathes to mills and CNC routers.

Age Requirements: 16+

When: Tuesdays and Fridays

Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Where: Rm. 1

LASER CUTTING

Get ready for an exciting Laser Cutting class, suitable for ages 16 and above! Learn how to unleash the power of laser technology to cut and engrave various materials, from wood and acrylic to leather and cardboard. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the process, from designing your creations to operating the laser cutter safely and efficiently.


Age Requirements: 16+

When: Saturdays

Time: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Where: Rm. 6
