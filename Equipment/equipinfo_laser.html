Equipment Reservation - LASER CUTTING

Our cutting-edge laser cutter is the ideal tool for those with precise and innovative projects. The latest laser cutter from LaserProInc is now on the market. Here's what you can anticipate from a laser cutting setup in a makerspace: Capabilities: Cutting: Laser cutters can accurately cut through various materials such as wood, acrylic, cardboard, fabric (depending on type), and some plastics. The thickness of materials that can be cut will vary based on the laser cutter's wattage. Engraving: The laser can also engrave designs or text onto material surfaces, creating a lasting mark. Process and Projects: Design Before Cutting: Makerspaces typically require you to design your project using software that generates a specific file format (often DXF or SVG) compatible with the laser cutter. Safety First: Laser cutters are powerful tools, and they are extremely hot, so wearing safety glasses is essential to shield your eyes from the laser beam. Our Makerspace requires additional safety training before using equipment. Unleash Your Creativity: With laser cutting, you can bring a wide range of ideas to life. People use them to create personalized gifts, custom jewelry, model parts, prototype inventions, and much more.
