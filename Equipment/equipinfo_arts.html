Equipment Reservation - ARTS and CRAFTS

InNOVALabs provides all equipment required for following activities:
  • Painting
  • Oil Pastels*
  • Color pencils
  • Realistic Drawing
  • Sewing Machines and Hand Stitching*
  • Origami*

- Painting Supplies: Makerspaces offer a wide range of painting supplies, such as acrylics, watercolors, and oils, allowing artists to experiment with different techniques and surfaces to bring their artistic visions to life. - Oil Pastels: Artists can utilize oil pastels as a vibrant and versatile medium to create textured artworks, with makerspaces providing a variety of colors for blending and layering to achieve unique effects. - Color Pencils: With precise control and vivid color saturation, color pencils are ideal for detailed illustrations and vibrant artworks, and makerspaces offer a wide selection of brands and techniques for artists to explore. - Sewing and Stitching: Makerspaces cater to sewing enthusiasts with equipment like sewing machines and hand stitching supplies, allowing makers to explore various techniques and fabrics for creating unique and functional pieces.
