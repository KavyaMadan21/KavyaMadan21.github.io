Membership

Signing up for membership gives you many abilities such as being able to sign up for events, reserve equipment, or reserve rooms.

Ranks

Depending on how much you pay, you are assigned a certain rank. Your rank decides how long you can use the facilities for and on what days.

  1. Novice Pioneer($10/month): Monday-Friday with the timings as stated on the calendar of events and hours page. Additionally, you can only reserve equipment or rooms for 3 days.
  2. Intermediate Developer($15/month): Monday-Friday and Saturday with the timings as stated on the calendar of events and hours page. Furthermore, you can only reserve equipment of rooms for 5 days.
  3. Master Innovator($20/month): 24/7 Monday-Friday and Saturday. Also, you can only reserve equipment or rooms for a week.
